About Us

Rob Leitschuh

Rob Leitschuh, CEO

I have been in the painting business for 45 years. In those years I have seen the changes in product use and the new innovative products available. Because I've worked on every style of house in New England, I know just where and when to use these new materials to give you the most beautiful paint job you can get.

Over these years I've worked on private homes from Victorian to Capes and Federal Houses to log homes that were 200-hundred years old, or brand new. I've also painted and repaired commercial buildings, offices, and schools. Because of this experience, I'm well versed in the problems and obstacles they may present to a perfect job. So, I know how to do the job right!

The Green Mountain Painting Crew

Our crew members are seasoned painters with a wide range of experience. They also know houses, paints, and are committed to our standards of excellence...and your satisfaction.
